Our Story

Built From the Ground Up

Titan Concrete Solutions was founded in 2008 by Marcus Reeves — a third-generation concrete professional who grew up on job sites, learning the craft from hands that had poured foundations for over five decades.

Today, Titan operates a fleet of 28 vehicles, employs 85+ skilled tradesmen, and has completed over 2,400 projects across commercial, industrial, and residential sectors. We don't subcontract our core work. Every pour is supervised by our own people, ensuring the quality and precision our name is built on.

Precision First

Tolerances measured in fractions. Every grade checked, every reinforcement verified.

Own Crews

No subcontractors for core work. Our trained teams handle every critical pour.

Full Warranty

Industry-leading warranty coverage on all structural and flatwork projects.

Safety Record

Zero lost-time incidents in the last 4 consecutive years. OSHA compliant.
